What it’s about

Want to develop your career in tech? Discover how to thrive as a leader and mentor.

Becoming a great engineer is one journey. Becoming a great leader of engineers is another entirely — and most of us are handed the second with almost no map. Tech Leadership is that map.

Drawing on decades of leadership at Instagram, Google, and YouTube, Andrew Swerdlow helps the next generation of entrepreneurs and innovators move from individual contributors to knowledgeable, effective managers and team leads. It’s an accessible guide to growing into a larger role — no matter your education or background.

The book is packed with real-life stories from years in the field, practical frameworks and tools you can apply immediately, and anecdotes from seasoned experts who have been where you are now.

You’ll learn to
  • Master the five critical elements of product development
  • Build your capacity to mentor — and multiply your impact through others
  • Support the success of the colleagues around you
  • Lead through collaboration, strategy, and execution
  • Borrow hard-won insights from the world’s leading tech companies
  • Make the leap from “great engineer” to “leader people follow”
Who it’s for

If you’re standing at the edge of leadership, this is your blueprint.

The new manager

You were the strongest engineer in the room — and now you’re responsible for the room. Learn the skills no one taught you.

The aspiring lead

You want the title, the scope, and the influence. Understand what leaders actually do, and how to start doing it now.

The founder & builder

You’re scaling a team and a product at once. Get frameworks for mentorship, strategy, and execution that hold up under pressure.

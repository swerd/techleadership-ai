Work With Me

A small number of engagements, chosen with care.

I keep a demanding full-time leadership role, and I like it that way — it means everything I share is current, hard-won, and tested at scale. It also means I take on only a handful of speaking, advising, and coaching engagements each year. If we work together, it’s because I believe I can make a genuine difference.

Talks that move rooms of technologists.

02 — Advising & Consulting

Trusted counsel for founders and engineering leaders.

A confidential thinking partner for the decisions that don’t have clean answers.

I advise a small portfolio of founders and leaders on org design, technical strategy, and the messy human realities of scaling people and product at the same time.

One-on-one, for the leap that defines a career.

Coaching for new and emerging tech leaders making the move from individual contributor to manager — and from manager to leader.

Engagements are intentionally limited and deeply personal. We work on the real situations in front of you, with the frameworks and emotional grounding to make lasting change.

  • The first 90 days as a manager
  • Growing influence beyond your title
  • Difficult conversations and decisions
  • Leading former peers
  • Building your own leadership voice
  • Cohort-based intensives
  • For aspiring and first-time leaders
  • Real frameworks, real practice
  • A community of ambitious peers
  • Guidance for founders and early teams

For the next generation of builders.

Intensive cohorts and mentorship for entrepreneurs and innovators ready to grow into bigger roles, faster.

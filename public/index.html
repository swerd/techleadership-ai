My Story

A career spent unlocking what teams are capable of.

I am a technology pioneer who has led cutting-edge software projects across many disciplines — today at the intersection of AI and large-scale infrastructure. Along the way I have been awarded more than thirty-five patents and helped build products used by billions of people.

What I care about most is people: caring for team members while unlocking productivity, and helping the next generation of leaders make lasting change. I mentor emerging leaders, advise founders, and co-founded The Laundry, a San Francisco experiences incubator.

The Book

Want to develop your career in tech? Discover how to thrive as a leader and mentor.

Drawing on leadership at Instagram, Google, and YouTube, Tech Leadership is the practical, accessible guide to growing into a larger role — no matter your education or background.

It is packed with real-life stories from years in the field, frameworks you can use on Monday morning, and hard-won wisdom from seasoned experts. The book equips you to master the five critical elements of product development, build your capacity to mentor, and lead through collaboration, strategy, and execution.
